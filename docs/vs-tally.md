# GitForms vs Tally - Comparison

> Why choose GitForms over Tally for your form needs?

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | GitForms | Tally |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **Price** | 100% Free | Freemium ($29/mo Pro) |
| **Privacy** | Zero tracking | Analytics and cookies |
| **Open Source** | MIT License | Closed source |
| **Self-Hosted** | Your GitHub | Their servers |
| **Data Ownership** | 100% yours | Their database |
| **MCP Server** | Official | Third-party only |

## Pricing

**GitForms**: Completely free. Forever. No tiers, no limits, no credit card.

**Tally**: Free tier with 100 submissions/form limit. Pro at $29/month.

## Privacy

**GitForms**: Zero tracking pixels. No analytics. No cookies. Data in YOUR GitHub.

**Tally**: Uses analytics, cookies, and stores data on their servers.

## Developer Experience

**GitForms**: MCP server for Claude Code, natural language management, open source.

**Tally**: No MCP support, drag-and-drop builder, API on paid plans only.

## Conclusion

GitForms for **developers and privacy-conscious users**.
Tally for **non-technical users** who need no-code building.
